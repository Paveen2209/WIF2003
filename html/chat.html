What should I eat after swimming?
After swimming, eat lean protein (like chicken or tofu), whole grains (like rice or quinoa), and a hydrating snack (like fruit or yogurt) to refuel and recover.
How many many grams of protein should I eat after swimming?
After swimming, aim for 10-20 grams of protein to support muscle recovery. This can be achieved with a protein shake, Greek yogurt, or a small serving of chicken or fish.
