Veggie Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables and add tomato sauce.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce and top with Parmesan.
  4. Serve hot.

Calories: 400
