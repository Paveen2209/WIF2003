Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • Chopped vegetables (cucumber, tomatoes, bell peppers)
  • Olive oil and vinegar dressing

Instructions:

  1. Mix quinoa and vegetables.
  2. Add dressing and toss well.
  3. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Calories: 320
