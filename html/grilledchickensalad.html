Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 200g grilled chicken breast
  • 100g mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber slices
  • Olive oil and lemon dressing

Instructions:

  1. Grill the chicken breast and slice it.
  2. Mix salad greens, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber in a bowl.
  3. Top with grilled chicken slices.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon dressing.
  5. Serve immediately.

Calories: 350
