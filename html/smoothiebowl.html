Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Granola and fresh fruit for topping

Instructions:

  1. Blend frozen berries, banana, and yogurt until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with granola and fresh fruit.
  3. Serve chilled.

Calories: 300
